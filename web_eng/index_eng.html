About Us

Hi, we are 'Música Jovem'

The 'Música Jovem' project started in 2020 with 4 young musicians to provide and demonstrate the passion we all have for Music. We do not have a fixed repertoire constantly varying from eras and composers, from the smallest to famous composers like Wolfang Amadeus Mozart and Christian Johann Bach.

Info Sheet

Services

Concerts

We perform concerts anywhere in the country and abroad, with the most flexible repertoire as desired.

Weddings

We perform weddings anywhere in the country, with the religious repertoire or not, at the decision of the engaged couple.

Other Events

We also hold other events such as silver or bronze anniversaries, weddings reception and birthdays.
